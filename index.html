About Me

I am a postdoc researcher at S-Lab for Advanced Intelligence, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), supervised by Prof. Cheng Long. Before that, I was a PhD student at the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering, Hunan University (HNU), advised by Prof. Dong Wang and Prof. Zhu Xiao. And I was a research intern at Zhejiang Lab, supervised by Prof. Hongyang Chen.

My research interests are broadly in data mining and deep learning. Specifically, I focus on spatio-temporal data mining and trajectory computing.
